Magical islands


French Polynesia is composed of 118 islands and atolls. It's largest chain of islets worldwide, grouped into five archipelagoes.

Stunning mountain peaks, vivid turquoise waters, marine life diversity, and white-sand beaches is all what you are going to spot.

Exquisite turquoise lagoons


Go snorkeling or scuba diving to discover the most amazing coral reefs worldwide, surrounding submerged volcanoes.

Immerse yourself in pristine waters. You can even spot sea turtles and dolphins off the coast.

A romantic reality


It's the most romantic trip left on Earth, your perfect dreamy getaway.
A superb destination for a honeymoon, a lovers' tryst, or a simple but romantic vacation in the true paradise.

It's full of charming retreats and rewards that will make your dream vacation truly unforgettable.

Tahitian culture


Tahitians base their culture on a 
phylosophy known as "aita pea pea", 
meaning "not to worry".

Their islands are all embraced by mana: a life force and spirit that surrounds and connects all living things. It's a spiritual quality of a supernatural origin.
